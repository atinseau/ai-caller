End the current phone call. Use this tool when:
- The caller explicitly says goodbye, thanks you and indicates they are done, or asks to hang up.
- You have resolved all the caller's questions and they confirm they don't need anything else.
- The caller is unresponsive after two or more attempts to re-engage.

Before calling this tool, always confirm: "Is there anything else I can help you with?" If they say no, say a brief warm goodbye, then invoke this tool.

Do NOT use this tool if:
- The conversation is still active and the caller has unanswered questions.
- The caller has not confirmed they want to end the call.
- You are in the middle of processing a tool result the caller is waiting for.
